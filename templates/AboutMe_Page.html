About Me

This was posted on the 12th of April 2015

For as long as I can remember I have had a passion for technology and later discovered at the start of secondary school that I had a passion for business too. Naturally, this passion for both business and technology led to me taking double business and IT for my GCSE options. After getting an A in both these subjects, I then took this a step forward in taking both double business and IT at level where I received two A*'s for Business Studies and a A* for IT. Deciding I want to take this passion forward, I decided to take a 4 year course at Coventry University studying Business Information Technology which blends both of my passions together into one course.

Outside of my work life, I enjoy several things. One of these main things is gaming; remembering the fist game I ever bought which was Guitar Hero 3: Legends of rock. I take pride in having played the guitar hero series for 8 years now, honing my skills through each instrument. If i'm not gaming, I will either be listening to music, reading or socialising with my friends. I also enjoy being kept in the loop with what is happening in the world so I check the news on a daily basis.

Also being a bit of a nerd or geek when it comes to technology, i'm always aware of the new and developing technologies that are coming from around the world, and hope one day to build my own gaming PC once I have the money for it.
